# CRM Application - Marketing Website Content

## Product Overview
- Professional relationship management platform for individuals and teams
- Comprehensive contact, interaction, and relationship tracking in one unified system
- Modern, intuitive interface with powerful visualization tools
- Privacy-focused with complete data ownership and export capabilities

## Core Value Propositions

### Relationship Intelligence
- **Network Visualization**: See your entire relationship network at a glance with interactive 2D and 3D graph views
- **Relationship Hierarchy**: Automatically rank contacts by relationship value (Partners, Friends, Connections, etc.)
- **Interaction Timeline**: Track every meeting, call, email, and event with each person
- **Relationship Notes**: Add context and history to every relationship

### Contact Management
- **Smart Contact Organization**: Tag, group, and categorize contacts for easy discovery
- **Infinite Scroll People List**: Effortlessly browse through thousands of contacts
- **Advanced Search**: Global search bar for instant access to anyone in your network
- **Profile Pages**: Rich profiles with contact info, social accounts, interaction history, and relationship details

### Social Profile Integration
- **Multi-Platform Tracking**: Link Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, and other social accounts to contacts
- **Follower/Following Management**: Track social connections alongside your CRM relationships
- **Account Consolidation**: View all social personas for each person in one place
- **Profile Analytics**: Understand your network's online presence

### Interaction Tracking
- **Comprehensive Logging**: Record meetings, calls, emails, and custom interaction types
- **Group Interactions**: Log interactions involving multiple people and groups simultaneously
- **Rich Context**: Add descriptions, dates, images, and attachments to every interaction
- **Timeline View**: Visualize relationship progression through interaction history

### Group Management
- **Flexible Groups**: Create project teams, interest groups, or any organizational structure
- **Bidirectional Membership**: Easy member management from both group and person perspectives
- **Group Interactions**: Track group-level interactions and events
- **Customizable Tags**: Label groups with custom tags for easy identification

### Data Management
- **XML Import/Export**: Full backup and migration of your entire database
- **CSV Import**: Bulk import contacts from Google Contacts and other sources
- **Data Ownership**: Your data stays with you - no vendor lock-in
- **Privacy-First**: ME user data excluded from exports, complete control over information sharing

### API & Integration
- **REST API**: Full programmatic access to your CRM data
- **API Keys**: Secure, granular API key management for integrations
- **Third-Party Access**: Enable external apps to read and write CRM data
- **API Documentation**: Interactive playground with live code examples

### Customization & Settings
- **Custom Relationship Types**: Define your own relationship categories with colors and hierarchy values
- **Custom Interaction Types**: Create interaction categories specific to your workflow
- **User Settings**: Customize authentication, profile information, and preferences
- **SSO Support**: Enterprise single sign-on for team deployments
